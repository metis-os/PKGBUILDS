About

METIS Linux: Super minimal and resource friendly Linux distribution, focusing on the the runit init system.

METIS is a lightweight distribution which can operate with less than 300MB of memory. It utilizes window managers and a selection of lightweight applications.
METIS comes pre-installed with two window managers: EWM(floating wm) and DWM(tiling wm). The default window manager is DWM, but additional pre-configured window managers can be explicitly installed, such as metis-bspwm.
Featuring a range of flat and pastel colors, consistent UI elements, and diverse themes for each window manager, METIS offers a unique, clean, minimal, and breathtaking visual experience.
It also includes built-in support for AUR, allowing for powerful customization and giving you the freedom to tailor your Linux PC to your liking.
